KEOPS CLEANING LLC.

At KEOPS CLEANING LLC we are proud to offer you an exceptional carpet and furniture cleaning service that will completely transform your spaces. We specialize in cleaning carpets and upholstered furniture affected by pets and stains caused by accidents, or simply if you want to maintain your spaces.

Register now for a free video consultation with one of our specialists or send us the images so we can determine the best possible cost, we are happy to help you with your carpet.

Why choose us?

We are a family business with years of experience in general household cleaning, now we have our line specialized in cleaning carpets and upholstered furniture where we strive to provide the best service at the best price.

We work for the areas of New Jersey and Pennsylvania, Support a small business and become our new client! You will not regret!

Professional Experience: We have a highly trained team with years of experience in carpet care and cleaning. We use proven methods and cutting-edge technology to guarantee impeccable results.

Advanced Technology: We remain at the forefront of the industry, using state-of-the-art equipment and cleaning products. This ensures a deep clean that removes not only visible dirt, but also particles and allergens trapped in the carpet fibers.

Personalized Service: We understand that each customer and each rug are unique. We customize our services to meet your specific needs, ensuring we exceed your expectations.

We use effective and safe cleaning products for your home and nature. Our philosophy is to provide extraordinary results without compromising the health of your family or the environment.

Restoration and Renewal: We not only clean, but also strive to restore and renew your carpets. Our goal is to prolong their useful life and restore them to their original splendor.
